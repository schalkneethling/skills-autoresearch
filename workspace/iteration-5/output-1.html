Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FlowBoard. Can't find what you're looking for? Our support team is happy to help.

Getting Started

  • How do I create my first board in FlowBoard?

    After signing in, click the + New Board button on your dashboard. Give your board a name, choose a template or start from scratch, then click Create Board. Your board is ready to use immediately — you can add columns, cards, and teammates straight away.

  • How do I invite teammates to my workspace?

    Go to Settings → Members and click Invite people. Enter one or more email addresses separated by commas, select a role (Admin, Member, or Viewer), and click Send invitations. Invitees will receive an email with a link to join your workspace.

  • Can I import data from Trello or Asana?

    Yes. Go to Settings → Import, select your source tool, and follow the steps to authorise access or upload an export file. FlowBoard will map boards, lists, cards, and assignments automatically. Imports typically complete within a few minutes depending on the size of your data.

  • Is there a mobile app available?

    FlowBoard has native apps for iOS (14+) and Android (10+). Search for "FlowBoard" in the App Store or Google Play and install the free app. Your boards, comments, and notifications sync in real time across all your devices.

Billing & Plans

  • What plans does FlowBoard offer?

    FlowBoard has three plans:

    • Free – up to 3 boards and 5 members, unlimited cards.
    • Pro ($12/user/month) – unlimited boards, advanced automations, and priority support.
    • Business ($28/user/month) – everything in Pro plus SSO, audit logs, and a dedicated customer success manager.

    All paid plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.

  • How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

    Go to Settings → Billing → Change plan. Select the plan you want and confirm. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfer for annual Business plan contracts. All transactions are processed securely via Stripe. We do not store card details on our servers.

  • Can I get a refund if I cancel my subscription?

    We offer a full refund within 30 days of your initial purchase if you're not satisfied. After 30 days, subscription fees are non-refundable, but you will retain access to paid features until the end of your billing period. To request a refund, contact billing@flowboard.io.

Integrations

  • Which tools can I connect to FlowBoard?

    FlowBoard integrates with over 50 tools, including Slack, Microsoft Teams, GitHub, GitLab, Jira, Figma, Google Drive, Dropbox, Zapier, and Webhooks for custom workflows. Browse the full list under Settings → Integrations.

  • How do I connect FlowBoard to Slack?

    Go to Settings → Integrations → Slack and click Connect to Slack. You'll be redirected to Slack to authorise access. Once connected, choose which Slack channels should receive notifications for card updates, comments, and due date reminders. You can adjust notification rules at any time.

  • Does FlowBoard have a public API?

    Yes. FlowBoard offers a REST API available on the Pro and Business plans. Full documentation, authentication guides, and code examples are available at developers.flowboard.io. API rate limits are 1,000 requests per minute per workspace. Webhooks are also available for event-driven integrations.

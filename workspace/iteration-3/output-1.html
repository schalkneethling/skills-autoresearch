Frequently Asked Questions

Browse answers to common questions about FlowBoard. Can't find what you're looking for? Our support team is here to help.

Getting Started

  • How do I create my first FlowBoard project?

    After signing up, click New Project in the top-right corner of your dashboard. Give it a name and choose a template — we offer Kanban, Scrum sprint, and simple to-do layouts. You can always change the layout later from Project Settings.

  • How do I invite teammates to a project?

    Open your project and go to Settings › Members. Enter your teammate's email address and choose their role (Viewer, Editor, or Admin). They'll receive an invitation email with a link to join.

    If your plan includes SSO, you can also auto-provision members from your identity provider.

  • Can I import tasks from Trello, Jira, or a CSV file?

    Yes. Go to Settings › Import and select your source. FlowBoard supports direct imports from Trello boards and Jira projects, and accepts CSV files in a standard format. Download our CSV template to see the required columns.

  • Is there a mobile app?

    FlowBoard is available on iOS and Android. Search for "FlowBoard" in the App Store or Google Play. The mobile app supports viewing and editing tasks, leaving comments, and receiving push notifications for mentions and due-date reminders.

Billing & Plans

  • What's included in the free plan?

    The free plan includes up to 3 projects, 10 members, and 100 tasks per project. File attachments are limited to 5 MB each. Integrations and advanced automation are available on paid plans only.

  • How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

    Go to Account › Billing › Change Plan. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated to your current billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period — your access continues until then.

  • Do you offer discounts for non-profits or education?

    Yes — verified non-profits and accredited educational institutions receive 50% off all paid plans. Email discounts@flowboard.io with proof of eligibility and we'll apply the discount to your account within two business days.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) via Stripe. Annual plan customers can also pay by bank transfer or purchase order — contact billing@flowboard.io to arrange this.

Integrations

  • Which integrations does FlowBoard support?

    FlowBoard connects natively with Slack, GitHub, GitLab, Google Drive, Figma, and Zapier. Via Zapier you can connect to thousands of additional tools. A full list is available in the Integrations directory.

  • How do I connect FlowBoard to Slack?

    Go to Settings › Integrations › Slack and click Connect. You'll be redirected to Slack to authorise the app. Once connected, choose which channel should receive task notifications and which events should trigger them (new tasks, status changes, comments, due-date reminders).

  • Can I create FlowBoard tasks from a GitHub pull request?

    Yes. After connecting the GitHub integration, you can link a PR to an existing task or create a new task directly from a PR using the /flowboard slash command in a PR comment. The task will automatically update its status when the PR is merged or closed.

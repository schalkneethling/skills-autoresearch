Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FlowBoard. Can't find what you're looking for? Our support team is here to help.

Getting Started

How do I create my first project in FlowBoard?

After signing in, click New Project in the top-left sidebar. Give your project a name and optional description, then choose a template (Kanban, Scrum sprint, or blank board) to get started. Your board is ready immediately — invite teammates from the project's Members tab.

How do I invite teammates to my workspace?

Go to Settings → Workspace → Members and click Invite people. Enter one or more email addresses (comma-separated) and select a role: Viewer, Editor, or Admin. Invitees will receive an email with a link valid for 7 days.

If you're on the Free plan, you can invite up to 5 members. Upgrade to Pro or Business for unlimited seats.

Can I import tasks from Trello, Jira, or a CSV file?

Yes. Navigate to Settings → Integrations → Import and select your source. FlowBoard supports direct imports from Trello (via JSON export) and Jira (via CSV export). You can also import any CSV file using our column-mapping wizard — download the sample template to see the expected format.

What browsers and devices does FlowBoard support?

FlowBoard works in the latest two versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Mobile browsers on iOS 16+ and Android 12+ are fully supported. Native iOS and Android apps are available on the downloads page.

Billing & Plans

What's included in the Free plan?

The Free plan includes up to 3 active projects, 5 workspace members, 250 MB of file storage, and basic integrations (Slack and GitHub). Boards, cards, labels, and due dates are all available at no cost.

How do I upgrade, downgrade, or cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings → Billing → Plan. From there you can switch between Free, Pro ($12/user/month), and Business ($28/user/month) plans, or cancel at any time. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle; cancellations preserve access until the period ends.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or educational institutions?

Yes — verified nonprofits receive 50% off all paid plans, and accredited educational institutions receive free Pro access for up to 30 members. Apply for a discount with your organisation's documentation; approvals typically take 2–3 business days.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your projects and data remain accessible in read-only mode for 30 days after cancellation. During this window you can export everything via Settings → Export workspace. After 30 days, data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

Integrations

How do I connect FlowBoard to Slack?

Go to Settings → Integrations → Slack and click Connect. You'll be redirected to Slack to authorise the FlowBoard app. Once connected, choose which Slack channels receive notifications for card assignments, due-date reminders, and status changes.

Can I link pull requests and commits from GitHub or GitLab?

Yes. After connecting your GitHub or GitLab account in Settings → Integrations, reference a FlowBoard card ID (e.g., FB-142) in a commit message or PR title. The card will automatically show the linked PR and its status — open, merged, or closed.

Is there a REST API or webhook support?

FlowBoard provides a full REST API and outbound webhooks on Pro and Business plans. You can create cards, update statuses, and query project data programmatically. Full documentation, code examples, and a Postman collection are available in the developer portal.

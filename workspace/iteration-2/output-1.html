Frequently Asked Questions

Browse answers to common questions about FlowBoard. Use the search below to find what you need.

Getting Started

  • How do I create my first board?

    After signing in, click New Board in the top-right corner of your dashboard. Give your board a name, choose a template or start blank, then click Create. Your board will open immediately and you can start adding cards.

  • How do I invite teammates to my workspace?

    Go to Settings → Members and click Invite People. Enter one or more email addresses separated by commas, choose a role (Admin, Member, or Viewer), then click Send Invites.

    Invitees receive an email with a link to join. Pending invitations are listed under the Members tab until accepted.

  • Can I import data from another project management tool?

    Yes. FlowBoard supports importing from Trello (JSON export), Asana (CSV export), and Jira (XML export). Navigate to Settings → Import & Export, select your source, and follow the on-screen steps.

  • Is there a mobile app available?

    FlowBoard is available as a native app for iOS and Android. Search for "FlowBoard" in the App Store or Google Play. Your boards, cards, and comments sync in real time across all your devices.

Billing & Plans

  • What plans does FlowBoard offer?

    FlowBoard offers three plans:

    • Free — Up to 3 boards and 5 members, unlimited cards.
    • Pro ($12/user/month) — Unlimited boards, advanced automations, and priority support.
    • Enterprise — Custom pricing with SSO, audit logs, and a dedicated account manager.

    You can compare all features on the pricing page.

  • How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

    Go to Settings → Billing and click Change Plan. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated to your current billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.

  • Do you offer a free trial for the Pro plan?

    Yes — all new workspaces get a 14-day free trial of Pro automatically. No credit card is required to start. You'll receive a reminder 3 days before the trial ends, at which point you can subscribe or continue on the Free plan.

  • How do I get a refund?

    We offer a full refund within 30 days of your first charge if you're not satisfied. Contact billing@flowboard.io with your workspace name and the reason for the request. Refunds are typically processed within 5–7 business days.

Integrations

  • Which tools can I connect to FlowBoard?

    FlowBoard integrates with Slack, GitHub, GitLab, Figma, Google Drive, Dropbox, Zapier, and more. A full list is available in Settings → Integrations. New integrations are added regularly.

  • How do I connect FlowBoard to Slack?

    In FlowBoard, go to Settings → Integrations → Slack and click Connect. You'll be redirected to Slack to authorise access. Once connected, you can choose which board activity (new cards, due-date reminders, comments) triggers a Slack notification and which channel receives it.

  • Can I use the FlowBoard API to build my own integration?

    Yes. FlowBoard provides a REST API and webhook support. Full documentation, code samples, and an interactive API explorer are available at flowboard.io/developers. API access is available on the Pro and Enterprise plans.

Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I create a new FlowBoard workspace?

After signing up, click the "Create Workspace" button on your dashboard. Give your workspace a name, invite team members by email, and choose a starter template or start from scratch. Your workspace will be ready in seconds.

Can I import tasks from other project management tools?

Yes. FlowBoard supports direct imports from Trello, Asana, Jira, and Monday.com. Go to Settings > Import Data, select your source tool, and follow the guided steps. CSV imports are also available for other tools.

What browsers and devices does FlowBoard support?

FlowBoard works on the latest two versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We also offer native apps for iOS and Android. All plans include full mobile access.

How do I invite team members to my workspace?

Open your workspace settings and select "Members." Enter the email addresses of the people you want to invite, assign them a role (Admin, Editor, or Viewer), and click "Send Invitations." They will receive an email with a link to join.

Billing & Plans

What plans does FlowBoard offer?

We offer three plans: Free (up to 5 users, 3 boards), Pro ($9/user/month, unlimited boards, advanced automations), and Enterprise (custom pricing with SSO, audit logs, and a dedicated account manager). All paid plans include a 14-day free trial.

How do I cancel or downgrade my subscription?

Go to Settings > Billing > Manage Plan. You can downgrade to a lower tier or cancel at any time. When you cancel, your workspace remains accessible in read-only mode until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a full refund within the first 30 days of any new paid subscription. After that, you can cancel at any time and your plan will remain active until the end of the billing cycle, but we are unable to issue partial refunds.

Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?

Yes. Visit Settings > Billing and click "Switch to Annual" or "Switch to Monthly." Annual plans save you 20%. When switching, we prorate the remaining balance on your current plan.

Integrations

Which third-party apps can I connect to FlowBoard?

FlowBoard integrates with over 50 tools including Slack, GitHub, GitLab, Google Drive, Dropbox, Figma, and Zapier. Browse the full list in Settings > Integrations.

How do I set up the Slack integration?

Go to Settings > Integrations > Slack and click "Connect." You will be asked to authorize FlowBoard in your Slack workspace. Once connected, you can choose which boards send notifications and to which channels.

Does FlowBoard have a public API?

Yes. Our REST API lets you create, read, update, and delete boards, tasks, and comments programmatically. API keys are available on all Pro and Enterprise plans. Visit our developer documentation at developers.flowboard.example.com for details.
